Privacy and Cookies Policy

Introduction

At our company, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you browse our website and use our services. It also details our use of cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. These cookies contain information that allows us to recognize your device on future visits, facilitating navigation and improving site functionality. We use cookies to personalize your experience, analyze usage patterns, and tailor our content to your interests.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the basic operation of the site and allow you to navigate and use its features. Without these cookies, certain services would not be available.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how users interact with our website by collecting anonymous data about visits and behavior. These cookies allow us to improve the site's performance and usability.
  • Functionality Cookies: These provide a more personalized experience by remembering your preferences, such as language or region. Thanks to these cookies, you don't need to reconfigure these settings each time you visit.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to display relevant ads based on your interests. These cookies help track the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and limit the number of times you see an ad.

How to Control and Delete Cookies

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or notify you when a cookie is being sent. Note that if you choose to disable certain cookies, some features of our site may be affected. Please refer to your browser's help section for information on managing your cookie preferences.
